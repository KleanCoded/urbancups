The Process



Step 1

Hover These Cards

Bring Your Cup


Come to the participating store with your cup, or purchase the cup at the counter



Step 2

Hand In Your Cup


Hand in your coffee cup and tell the cashier the cup's registered name

Step 3

Cleaning the cup


The store employees insert your cup into the cleaning machine. The cleaning process takes just 30 seconds!

Step 4

Coffee


The store employees will then fill your claned cup with coffee

Step 5

Enjoy your coffee!


Your coffee is ready in less than 2 minutes (approximately). The employees use the registered cup name to ensure you get your cup back.
